## 🫧 What was the Frutiger Aero Era?

**Frutiger Aero** was the dominant design language across technology, media, and architecture roughly between **2004 and 2013**. Named retroactively after font designer Adrian Frutiger and the iconic Windows Aero interface, it stood as a bridge between foundational digital abstractions and modern computing layout frameworks.

Characterized by complex skeuomorphism, vivid color palettes (primarily aquatic blues, vibrant greens, and orange accents), high-gloss textures, glass textures (`Aero Glass`), lens flares, and natural motifs (water droplets, green grass, bubbles), it represented an era of extreme technological optimism.
